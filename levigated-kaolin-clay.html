Levigated Kaolin Clay
  • Properties
  • Technical Specification
  • Packing Sizes

Kaolin clay is a natural mineral found in partly decomposed granite, an igneous feldsparic rock. Chemically, kaolin is a hydrated aluminium silicate containing Al2O3.2SiO2.2H2O.It is a unique industrial mineral, which remains chemically inert over a relatively wide pH range and it offers excellent covering when used as a pigment or extender in coated films and filling applications.
