Ball Clay


  • Properties
  • Packing Sizes

Ball Clay commonly consist of Kaolinite, Mica & Quartz.  Ball clay is a mineral formed from the weathering and transportation by water of parent rocks which are deposited in ancient river basins from where it is extracted.

It has many important properties such as High plasticity, High unfired strength, Highly controlled rheological properties, Controlled organic content, Controlled residue, Excellent workability.
