About Pavansut Minerals

Our Group introduces as a leading manufacturing organization which produce refined Soda Feldspar,Potash Feldspar, Kaolin Clay and many more by the technocrats having more than 20 years of experience in Ceramic Industry.

Our processing plant is located at Morbi(Ceramic City of India). We are equipped with all the facilities like handely picked feldspar from mines, crushing, grinding, refining, filtration, chemical & physical test laboratories by means of which we can have better control over the quality as well as quantity parameters in our product.
